The Story Behind Skilliest

From confusion and expensive rejections to securing 10+ fully funded PhD offers worldwide.

Starting from Scratch

I am Waleed Abdullah, an Electrical Engineer from Lahore, Pakistan. When I first considered studying abroad, I had absolutely no proper guidance. The entire process seemed like an overwhelming, expensive maze.

Like many applicants, I thought the only way to succeed was to hire a traditional admission consultant. I paid them, trusted their generic "university shortlisting," and soon realized they had no idea how funding actually worked, nor did they understand the nuances of my specific research field. They didn't tell me about professor outreach or fee waivers.

The Master's Admissions Journey

In 2023, I applied to over 15 top MS programs in the US and was accepted into almost all of them (University of Washington, Michigan Ann Arbor, Penn State, Ohio State, etc.). However, I faced a massive roadblock: Master's programs cost upwards of $120K with minimal funding available.

I realized that paying for a Master's was not viable for me. I needed full funding, and I discovered that the vast majority of university funding is allocated to PhD students through Research Assistantships (RAs).

Discovering the Power of Outreach

I pivoted my strategy entirely toward direct PhD admissions right after my Bachelor's degree. I learned the hard way that applying directly through official university portals without talking to professors is a trap. You waste $75–$150 per application, hoping a professor might see your file.

The real secret? Funding follows professors.

I began aggressively cold emailing professors worldwide. To scale this, I built an AI-powered outreach system that allowed me to collect thousands of professor contacts, generate highly personalized emails based on their research, and automatically follow up with them.

The Results

Through this system, I sent over 1000 emails. My approach completely changed my life. I successfully secured:

  • 10+ Fully Funded PhD Offers in the United States.
  • A Fully Funded PhD Offer at KFUPM in Saudi Arabia.
  • The highly prestigious Erasmus Mundus Scholarship in Europe.
  • All achieved directly with just a Bachelor's degree.

Lessons Learned

The admissions journey taught me critical lessons about what actually works and why most students fail.

❌ Why Most Students Fail

  • Applying blindly to university portals without verifying if professors have funding or capacity.
  • Wasting thousands of dollars on generic consultants, application fees, and unnecessary test scores.
  • Sending a single cold email and giving up when they don't get a reply.
  • Relying on just one or two applications instead of diversifying globally.

✅ What Actually Works

  • Consistent Outreach: Cold emailing is the best first step. It costs nothing and unlocks hidden funding opportunities.
  • Automated Follow-Ups: Professors are busy. The follow-up is where the conversion actually happens.
  • Risk Management: Diversifying applications across the US, Europe, and elsewhere to manage visa uncertainties.
  • Applying for Free: Leveraging application fee waivers to apply to 30+ programs without breaking the bank.

Why Skilliest Exists

I spent over $6,000 during my journey on test attempts, application fees, and bad advice. Most of this could have been optimized or avoided entirely with the right guidance.

I built the Skilliest Scholarship Network to be the exact platform I wish existed when I started. Our mission is to help students avoid years of confusion, save their hard-earned money, and focus their energy on highly optimized, proven techniques.
