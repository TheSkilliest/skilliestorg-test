The Story Behind Skilliest

Built by an applicant, for applicants. From confusion and expensive rejections to securing 10+ fully funded PhD offers worldwide.

Hi, I am Waleed Abdullah, an Electrical Engineer from Lahore, Pakistan. I built Skilliest after personally going through, navigating, and ultimately cracking the fully funded PhD admissions process myself.

When I first started, I had absolutely no proper guidance. Like many applicants, I paid a huge amount to a traditional admission consultant. I trusted their generic "university shortlisting," only to realize they had no idea how funding actually worked, nor did they understand my specific research field. They never mentioned professor outreach, and they didn't tell me about fee waivers.

🎓 Secured 10+ Fully Funded PhD Offers worldwide directly from my Bachelor's degree – without a Master's degree!

My Journey

2023

The Master's Misconception

Applied to 15+ top MS programs in the US and was accepted into almost all of them:

University of Washington University of Michigan Ann Arbor Penn State Ohio State Virginia Tech Northeastern

The Reality Check: Master's programs cost upwards of $120K with minimal funding. I could not get any fully funded offer for a master's program, and realized that most of the funding goes to the PhD programs. Hence, I started seeking a PhD admission and pivoted entirely.

2024

First Direct PhD Success

Secured a fully-funded PhD offer in Electrical Engineering at a top US university for Fall 2025 – directly from my Bachelor's degree!

The Lesson: I could not join because of the funding impact of the professor at the last moments. That's a hard lesson learned—not to rely on one single offer. I also learned the hard way that applying directly through official portals without talking to professors is a trap. The real secret? Funding follows professors.

2025

Aggressive Global Campaign & The AI Engine

I built an AI-powered outreach system to scale my efforts globally.

  • 1000+ professors contacted globally
  • 5000+ emails sent, including follow-up emails
  • 50+ PhD programs applied to (mostly with fee waivers)
  • 10+ fully-funded PhD offers secured

Diversification: Handled visa risks by diversifying applications across the US, Europe (Erasmus Mundus), and the Middle East (KFUPM).

2026

Current Status

  • 10+ Fully Funded PhD Offers in the US alone
  • Accepted the best offer and proceeding
  • Still receiving interview requests from professors
  • Built this community to turn my framework into the platform I wish existed when I started.

Amount I Spent in Finding The Right Path

$6,000+

Includes application fees, generic consultants, and test score sending to every school. I built Skilliest so you can optimize costs and avoid these mistakes.

The Skilliest Methodology

This network relies on specialized, field-tested strategies that we have mastered over years of real applications:

1. The AI Engine

Advanced systems for writing, sending, and automatically following up for bulk cold-emailing at scale.

2. Global Targeting

Framework for targeting the US, Canada, Australia, UK, and Europe simultaneously to mitigate visa risks.

3. Zero-Cost Applications

Advanced strategies for obtaining Application Fee Waivers to apply to top-tier programs for completely free.

4. Data Collection

AI-powered bulk data collection to identify global faculty contacts and deeply understand their specific research interests.

5. Elite Positioning

Crafting academic CVs, statements, and positioning profiles to stand out as an expert in the field.

6. Precision Timing

A master schedule for when to take English tests, request LORs, and launch the cold outreach campaigns.

Hard-Earned Lessons

❌ What Didn't Work

  • Applying blindly to university portals without talking to professors.
  • Wasting thousands on generic consultants who don't understand your specific research field.
  • Sending a single cold email and giving up.
  • Relying on just one or two applications instead of diversifying globally.
  • Paying for official test scores ($25+) to send to every single school.

✅ What Actually Worked

  • Aggressive professor outreach (1000+ emails sent globally).
  • Automated Follow-Ups: The follow-up is where the conversion actually happens.
  • Securing multiple fee waivers to apply for free.
  • Risk Management: Diversifying across the US, Europe, and Middle East.
  • Cost optimization at every single step.
